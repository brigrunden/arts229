ABOUT ME

I was always the student who turned in their assignments with drawings scribbled on them. Who am I kidding, I still do that.

My passion for art and design started young; and it hasn't stopped since the age of three when I first taught myself Microsoft Paint.

I started the Graphic Communications program at Lansing Community College after graduating high school in 2015, and have been given the opportunity to expand my abilities and learn new tools.

I am a 21 year-old graphic designer from Lansing, Michigan. Me, and my husband Jordan, enjoy spending our time together traveling, going to concerts & festivals and cooking.

I enjoy good design, large sweaters, Target, volunteering, frozen yogurt, Halloween, outdoor concerts, big cities, Christmas movies, when the leaves change colors, Netflix, thunderstorms, singing obnoxiously loud in the car, comfy chairs, shoes, carbs in any form, and iced coffee.

Most importantly, I don't trust anyone who hates The Office!

Let's talk about life!
